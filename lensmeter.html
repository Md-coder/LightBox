Lensmeter

LightBox

The lightbox Lensmeter adopts the advanced and new century style.With points made with safe nanomaterials that doesn't damage the lens, It has the distinctive functions of pupillary distance,UV and a fast action inbuilt printer. This machine has a blue light measurement that offers an intuitive user interface to enable the user to perform lens measurements simply and easily. It can also measure colorful and dark lenses at a very fast speed and in high accuracy.

Features

1. Adopts green measurement light beam allows to measure different kinds of dark lenses
2. Advanced sensor technology with 108 measurement points
3. Point is made by nanomaterials, which is no damage to the Lenses
4. Progressive measurement to improve accuracy and speed of near and far vision measurements
5. Auto recognition of single and progressive lens
6. All operations can be finished by key mode
