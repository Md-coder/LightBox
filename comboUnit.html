Combo Unit

LightBox

Lightbox combo Unit comes with human-oriented and automated designs. The combo unit is convenient to operate and easy to use. Features

1.Well designed and layout, Smooth movements and good practical functions.
2.Operated both by control panel and remote control.
3.Electric up/down chair, with rotate function.
4. Phoropter arm can be electric up/down, and can be rotated 90°in the highest.
5. Phoropter arm can attach manual vision tester or computerized vision tester.
6. Trial lens set can be placed inside of the table drawer.
7. The chair can be lifted up and down, rotated left and right, and with tiltable backrest for the comfortability of the patient
